Welcome to userism.net

We are the Userist Collective, a group willing to understand power in the digital era.
Scroll down to find out more...

Here is why:

User rise and the end of work

We are convinced that with digital, everything changes. Work, intended as phisical and mental effort to produce value, vanishes. Our needs and desires remain the same, but freed from most phisical costraints. We enter a truly human age.

The digital gesture and value we create

As every task gets automated and every product generated, a simple gesture becomes the source of power. We call it the digital gesture, such as a hand holding a device ‚Äî the moment where human intention meets digital potential. Without it, no technology has meaning, and no product carries any importance. Consumption becomes far more relevant than production.

The hand and the device

The smartphone is not just a tool ‚Äî it is a window onto an abstract world of ideas. The hand doesn't just hold it. It grips it, desires it, and controls it. Humans are at their most powerful in the digital age. But the immense power that comes with it, can go both ways, and even trough that window people remain vulnerable to hierarchies and to exploitation.

Courage and control

We believe that digital makes the case for a better life for everyone. However, only those that control digital can make that happen. That's why we believe it should be users right to own it. We should not be afraid of digital, neither of its consequences such as automation. Instead, we should try to own this transformation. As userocracy is put into place, benefits will unfold for everyone.

Finally, it's much more than digital alone. It's about economy, politics and society. As user, you are the engine of it all.
